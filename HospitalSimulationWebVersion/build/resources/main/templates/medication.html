Medications

Medication Abbreviation Description
Aspirin AS or As Aspirin, also known as acetylsalicylic acid, is a medication used to treat pain, fever, or inflammation.
Antibiotic AN or An An antibiotic is a type of antimicrobial substance active against bacteria and is the most important type of antibacterial agent for fighting bacterial infections.
Insulin I Insulin is a peptide hormone produced by beta cells of the pancreatic islets; it is considered to be the main anabolic hormone of the body.
Paracetamol P Paracetamol, also known as acetaminophen, is a medicine used to treat pain and fever. It is typically used for mild to moderate pain relief.
